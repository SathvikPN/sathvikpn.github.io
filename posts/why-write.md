# why write?

**why write when advent of AI abstracts it away**

i write,
i want to.
writing, even be it in draft state
is good enough for me to crystallize my thoughts.
i write to improve my own clarity of thoughts.

i write,
it slows me down but it helps me think when things are important
i treat it as an investment for things that are dear to me
i wish to be intellectually more active.
while i been trimming slop content across social media feeds,
i want to dedicate my attention currency for things that interests me.
i want to increase my threshold of focus,
this is one of my way of attempting to do so.
